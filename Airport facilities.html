Available Services

  • Flight Status: Real-time updates for arrivals and departures.
  • Lounges: Premium and business-class lounges with Wi-Fi, food, and relaxation zones.
  • Shopping: Duty-free stores, fashion, electronics, books, and souvenirs.
  • Dining: Food courts, cafes, gourmet restaurants, and quick bites.
  • Transportation: Taxi services, shuttle buses (Pushpak), car rentals, and parking.
  • Medical Center: 24/7 emergency medical services and pharmacies.
  • Wi-Fi: Free internet access for up to 4 hours.
  • Lost & Found: Assistance for misplaced luggage and personal belongings.
  • Forex & Banking: Currency exchange counters and ATMs.
  • Smoking Zones: Designated smoking areas within the airport.
  • Special Assistance: Wheelchair services, baby care rooms, and PRM (Persons with Reduced Mobility) support.
  • Business Services: Meeting rooms, co-working spaces, and business centers.
